Lemonade

Description

Lemonade is a refreshing, sweetened lemon-flavored beverage typically made with lemon juice, water, and a sweetener like sugar or honey.

ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ice
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Juice Your Lemons.
  2. Make a simple syrup with hot water to dissolve sugar.
  3. Stir.
  4. Add cold water.
  5. Serve.
